Official accommodation support is only available **from June 29**. Nights on 6/26, 6/27, 6/28 are outside official support.

In the master 참가자 tab, those pre-6/29 night cells are marked **`✓*`** (asterisk) instead of `✓`, and the affected rows carry a 비고 note: `* 6/29 이전 숙박(공식 숙소지원 외)`. As of v8, 11 participants are flagged (mostly 6/28; 한상환 from 6/26).

**How to apply:** When assigning rooms, treat pre-6/29 nights as special/non-official (may be self-pay or separate arrangement) — don't count them as standard supported nights. Keep the asterisk convention in any printable schedule. See [[printable-room-schedule]].
